Decision Control is the enterprise platform for managing business decisions as
governed, testable assets across their full lifecycle. Author and test decision
models in **DMN**, then promote them through environments with version control
and CI/CD for your business rules — compliance built in.

## Core capabilities

Decision Control brings together the people who own, build, and audit decisions
through three purpose-built interfaces:

## Governance & compliance

Decision Control treats every change as a governed event:

- **Multi-environment promotion** — isolated Development → Test/UAT → Production
  environments with governance checkpoints and standard, fast-track, or
  self-service approval workflows.
- **Four-eyes principle** — the creator of a change cannot approve their own
  change; separation of duties is enforced.
- **Complete audit trail** — every change records **who** made it, **when**,
  **what** changed, and **why** — regulator-ready documentation and the
  foundation of [Accountable AI](/faq/concepts/accountable-ai).

## Architecture

Decision Control is built on three pillars running on Kubernetes
infrastructure: the **Decision Control Tower** (management and governance), the
**Decision Control Application** (the core execution engine), and the
**Governance API**.

## Integration & deployment

- **REST APIs** for integrating decisions with existing applications.
- **Event-driven** processing via Kafka.
- **SSO** authentication through OAuth2/OIDC.
- **Kubernetes-native** deployment options.
