## Available Editions

### Decision Control Sandbox <span className="badge badge-new">Available Now</span>

Fast deployment for POCs, testing, and evaluation.

**Features**:

- 5-minute CloudFormation deployment
- Auto-login enabled for ease of use
- Perfect for demos and testing
- IP-restricted security model
- Cost-effective evaluation

**Best For**:

- Proof of concepts
- Development and testing
- Customer demonstrations
- Quick evaluation
