turning it into changes in a technical decision model. The **Aletyx AI Assistant**
closes that gap: it helps analysts understand complex DMN models, propose changes
in plain language, review them for edge cases, and generate test scenarios — while
keeping the analyst in control of the logic. The result is far less back-and-forth
with IT and faster, safer policy updates.
