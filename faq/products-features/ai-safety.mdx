title: "How does Aletyx keep AI safe in regulated decisions?"
description: "Aletyx applies deterministic AI guardrails so LLM outputs cannot override business policy, with every decision and workflow step captured in a full audit trail."

Aletyx applies **deterministic AI guardrails**. Rules are enforced
deterministically so that LLM outputs cannot override business policy or produce
unaudited decisions. Every decision and workflow step is captured in a complete
audit trail.
