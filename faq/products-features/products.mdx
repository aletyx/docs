description: "Aletyx offers Decision Intelligence for governed business logic and Intelligent Process Automation for compliant, auditable end-to-end workflows."

  systems, and enables safe AI integration through deterministic rule
  enforcement.
- **Intelligent Process Automation** — orchestrates end-to-end workflows while
  maintaining compliance and audit trails.
