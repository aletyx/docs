---
title: "Who is Aletyx for?"
description: "The audiences and use cases Aletyx serves."
---

Aletyx is designed for regulated industries and enterprises that need:

- Predictable, auditable decision outcomes
- Legacy system modernization without rewrites
- Safe AI integration that stays within business policies
- Compliance with governance requirements

It serves both business analysts (through visual authoring) and developers
(through SDKs, extensions, and APIs).
