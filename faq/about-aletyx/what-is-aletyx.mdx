---
title: "What is Aletyx?"
description: "Aletyx is an enterprise platform combining decision intelligence and process automation on Apache KIE (Drools, Kogito, jBPM), with governance and audit built in."
---

Aletyx is an enterprise automation platform that combines **decision
intelligence** and **intelligent process automation** with AI. It lets you model
business decisions, orchestrate end-to-end processes, and ship them to production
with governance and audit trails built in. The platform is built on enterprise
builds of the Apache KIE technologies — Drools, Kogito, and jBPM.
