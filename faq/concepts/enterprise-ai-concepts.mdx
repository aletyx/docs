To make sound architectural choices, enterprise leaders need a working grasp of
the main AI approaches — **Symbolic AI, Machine Learning, Generative AI, Large
Language Models, and Agentic AI** — and how they differ. AI is no longer
optional, but the decisions you make today determine whether AI initiatives
create lasting value or introduce new risk. The key is pairing innovation with
governance, explainability, and reliability rather than adopting technology for
its own sake.
