---
title: "How can enterprises adopt AI without losing compliance and predictability?"
description: "Hybrid Intelligence combines symbolic, generative, and agentic AI - using rule-based guardrails so enterprises can adopt AI without losing compliance or predictability."
---

Statistical AI like Generative AI is creative and disruptive, but its
unpredictability and lack of explainability make it unsafe for core operations
such as loan approvals or pricing. The answer is **Hybrid Intelligence** —
combining the transparency and control of Symbolic AI, the creative power of
GenAI, and the autonomy of Agentic AI. By using symbolic, rule-based systems as
guardrails around generative capabilities, enterprises can embrace AI innovation
without sacrificing governance or control of their business logic.
