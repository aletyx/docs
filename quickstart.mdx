## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have access to an Aletyx workspace and the
appropriate permissions to create and deploy models.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    From the dashboard, create a new project to hold your decisions and
    processes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Model a decision">
    Define your inputs, rules, and outputs. See [Decisions](/guides/decisions)
    for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and deploy">
    Validate your model against test cases, then deploy it to an environment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  You now have a deployed decision. Call it from the
  [API](/api-reference/introduction) to see it in action.
</Check>
