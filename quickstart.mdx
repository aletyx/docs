Aletyx serves different roles with different focus:

- **Decision analysts** model business decisions and processes visually,
  without writing code.
- **Tech leads** design the bigger picture: enterprise-scale automation
  systems that are secure and reliable.

Pick the path that matches how you work — each gets you to a working
result quickly.
