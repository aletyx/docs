How to get into the Control Tower and use the controls that are available from every screen - the Home page, the top masthead (About, theme, sign-out), and the collapsible sidebar.

![Decision Control Tower login screen](/images/decision-control-tower/login-screen.png)

## How to sign in with SSO

Single sign-on entry for any Tower user when the deployment is wired to an identity provider.

**Prerequisites:**

- A valid corporate account on the configured identity provider (Microsoft Entra or Keycloak).
- SSO must be enabled for your deployment.

**Steps:**

1. Open the Control Tower URL - you land on the Decision Control Tower login screen.
2. Click **Sign in with SSO**.
3. Complete authentication at your identity provider (the "select account" prompt).
4. Wait while the portal finalizes sign-in ("Authenticating, this may take 1 to 3 minutes").

**Verify:** You are redirected into the portal **Home** page and the top masthead appears.

## How to choose a service from the Home page

The Home page (`/`) is the portal's entry point after sign-in.

![Decision Control Tower Home](/images/decision-control-tower/home.png)

**Steps:**

1. From Home, read the hero title and pick one of the three service cards: **Environments**, **Promotions** or **Tasks**.
2. Click a card to open that area.

**Verify:** The selected area opens and the sidebar appears (the sidebar is hidden on Home).

## How to switch between light and dark theme

A personal display preference, saved in your browser.

**Prerequisites:** None.

**Steps:**

1. In the top masthead, click the **settings** (cog) menu.
2. Choose **Light** or **Dark** theme.

**Verify:** The interface switches palette immediately; the choice persists on reload.

## How to view the version (About)

Show the build version and commit identifier - useful when filing support tickets or bug reports.

**Prerequisites:** None.

**Steps:**

1. In the top masthead, click on the info icon.
2. Read **Build Version** and **Commit SHA** in the Decision Control Tower dialog.
3. Close the dialog.

**Verify:** The dialog displays the current build identifiers.


## How to collapse or expand the sidebar

Free up space on any screen that has a sidebar.

**Prerequisites:** Not on the Home page (Home has no sidebar).

**Steps:**

1. Click the sidebar **collapse/expand** toggle.

**Verify:** Collapsed, the sidebar shows icon-only items with tooltips; the state persists across reloads.
