A curated, vendor-neutral reading and viewing list for going deeper on business
decision and process modeling. Start with the standards, then pick a book and a
hands-on tool to practice with.

## Standards & specifications

The authoritative, freely available sources. These are the contracts that
DMN-, DRL-, and BPMN-compliant tools (including the [Aletyx Enterprise
Build](/tech-stack)) implement.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="DMN specification (OMG)" icon="file-lines" href="https://www.omg.org/spec/DMN/">
    The official Decision Model and Notation standard, including the FEEL
    expression language and decision table semantics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="BPMN specification (OMG)" icon="file-lines" href="https://www.omg.org/spec/BPMN/">
    The official Business Process Model and Notation standard defining process
    diagram elements and execution semantics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="bpmn.org" icon="diagram-project" href="https://www.bpmn.org/">
    OMG's BPMN portal with the quick-reference poster, examples, and a list of
    supporting tools.
  </Card>
  <Card title="DRL & rules (Apache KIE)" icon="gears" href="https://kie.apache.org/">
    Home of Drools, jBPM, and Kogito — the open source engines for DRL rules,
    DMN decisions, and BPMN processes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Learning DMN

Decision Model and Notation is the standard for modeling repeatable business
decisions with decision tables, decision requirements diagrams (DRDs), and FEEL.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="DMN Method and Style" icon="book" href="https://methodandstyle.com/dmn-training/">
    Bruce Silver's book and training — the practical reference for building
    correct, well-structured DMN models.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-World Decision Modeling with DMN" icon="book" href="https://methodandstyle.com/">
    Bruce Silver and James Taylor's deep-dive book on applying DMN to real
    business problems end to end.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trisotech — Learn DMN" icon="graduation-cap" href="https://www.trisotech.com/learn/">
    Free articles, examples, and tutorials on DMN, decision tables, and FEEL
    from a leading DMN tool vendor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Camunda DMN tutorial" icon="table-cells" href="https://camunda.com/dmn/">
    Approachable introduction to decision tables and DMN with hands-on
    examples.
  </Card>
  <Card title="What is DMN?" icon="circle-question" href="/faq/concepts/what-is-dmn">
    Aletyx's own primer on DMN and how it fits alongside DRL and BPMN.
  </Card>
  <Card title="DMN Playground" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/components/playground/dmn-tutorial">
    Build and test your first decision model interactively.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Learning BPMN

Business Process Model and Notation is the standard for modeling and executing
end-to-end business processes that orchestrate tasks, decisions, and people.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="BPMN Method and Style" icon="book" href="https://methodandstyle.com/bpmn-training/">
    Bruce Silver's book and training — the definitive guide to clear,
    executable BPMN.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-Life BPMN" icon="book" href="https://www.bpmnbook.com/">
    Jakob Freund and Bernd Rücker's pragmatic, example-driven introduction to
    modeling with BPMN (and DMN).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Camunda BPMN tutorial" icon="graduation-cap" href="https://camunda.com/bpmn/">
    A thorough, free online BPMN reference covering every symbol with examples.
  </Card>
  <Card title="bpmn.io" icon="code" href="https://bpmn.io/">
    Open source BPMN, DMN, and Forms toolkit — render and edit diagrams in the
    browser.
  </Card>
  <Card title="What is BPMN?" icon="circle-question" href="/faq/concepts/what-is-bpmn">
    Aletyx's own primer on BPMN and process automation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="BPMN Playground" icon="arrows-spin" href="/components/playground/bpmn-tutorial">
    Model and run your first process interactively.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
