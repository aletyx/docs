## 1. Supply Chain Management Process

### Business Scenario

A global electronics manufacturer optimizing its supply chain to reduce lead times and improve inventory management.

### Process Overview

![Diagram](/images/diagrams/guides/getting-started/use-cases/manufacturing/flowchart-td-1.svg)

### Key Process Details

| Stage                | Description                      | Duration  | Key Actors             |
| -------------------- | -------------------------------- | --------- | ---------------------- |
| Demand Forecasting   | Predict material requirements    | 1-2 weeks | Supply Chain Analysts  |
| Supplier Selection   | Evaluate and choose suppliers    | 1-2 weeks | Procurement Team       |
| Purchase Ordering    | Create and send purchase orders  | 3-5 days  | Purchasing Managers    |
| Logistics Management | Coordinate shipping and delivery | 2-4 weeks | Logistics Coordinators |

## 2. Quality Control Workflow

### Business Scenario

An automotive parts manufacturer implementing a comprehensive quality control process.

### Process Overview

![Diagram](/images/diagrams/guides/getting-started/use-cases/manufacturing/flowchart-td-2.svg)

### Key Process Details

| Stage               | Description                      | Duration  | Key Actors           |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------- | --------- | -------------------- |
| Initial Inspection  | Visual and preliminary checks    | 4-8 hours | Quality Technicians  |
| Detailed Testing    | Comprehensive quality assessment | 1-2 days  | Quality Control Team |
| Root Cause Analysis | Investigate quality issues       | 3-5 days  | Engineering Team     |
| Process Improvement | Implement corrective actions     | 1-2 weeks | Process Engineers    |

## 3. Inventory Management Process

### Business Scenario

A pharmaceutical manufacturing company developing an advanced inventory tracking and management system.

### Process Overview

![Diagram](/images/diagrams/guides/getting-started/use-cases/manufacturing/flowchart-td-3.svg)

### Key Process Details

| Stage                  | Description                     | Duration   | Key Actors            |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------- | ---------- | --------------------- |
| Inventory Tracking     | Real-time stock monitoring      | Continuous | Inventory Managers    |
| Reorder Management     | Automated and manual restocking | 3-7 days   | Procurement Team      |
| Inventory Optimization | Strategic inventory planning    | 1-2 weeks  | Supply Chain Analysts |
| Reporting              | Performance and trend analysis  | Monthly    | Management Team       |


## Business Value Proposition

These use cases demonstrate how process automation can:
- Reduce inventory carrying costs by 20-40%
- Improve supply chain visibility and transparency
- Enhance quality control accuracy
- Minimize production delays
- Optimize resource allocation
- Provide real-time tracking and reporting
- Accelerate decision-making processes
- Ensure regulatory compliance
- Reduce human error in critical manufacturing processes
