## 1. Order Fulfillment Process

### Business Scenario

An e-commerce retailer optimizing its order processing and fulfillment workflow to improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

### Key Process Details

| Stage                  | Description                       | Duration   | Key Actors           |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------- | -------------------- |
| Order Processing       | Initial order validation          | 1-2 hours  | Sales System         |
| Inventory Verification | Stock availability check          | 30-60 mins | Inventory Management |
| Picking and Packing    | Product selection and preparation | 2-4 hours  | Warehouse Team       |
| Shipping               | Carrier handoff and tracking      | 1-2 days   | Logistics Team       |

## 2. Customer Complaint Resolution

### Business Scenario

A multi-channel retailer developing a comprehensive customer service resolution process.

### Process Overview

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/uc-retail-flow-2.svg" alt="Flowchart of a customer complaint handling process: Complaint Submission through various channels, Automated Initial Triage, then routing by complaint type to Customer Service, Store Manager, Technical Support, Customer Experience or Finance, with resolution paths such as Product Exchange, Financial Compensation or Service Scheduling, ending with a satisfaction check" className="block dark:hidden" />
  <img src="/images/uc-retail-flow-2-dark.svg" alt="Flowchart of a customer complaint handling process: Complaint Submission through various channels, Automated Initial Triage, then routing by complaint type to Customer Service, Store Manager, Technical Support, Customer Experience or Finance, with resolution paths such as Product Exchange, Financial Compensation or Service Scheduling, ending with a satisfaction check" className="hidden dark:block" />

### Key Process Details

| Stage          | Description                 | Duration   | Key Actors          |
| -------------- | --------------------------- | ---------- | ------------------- |
| Initial Triage | Complaint categorization    | 30-60 mins | Customer Service    |
| Investigation  | Root cause analysis         | 1-2 days   | Relevant Department |
| Resolution     | Implementing solution       | 2-5 days   | Support Teams       |
| Follow-up      | Customer satisfaction check | 7-14 days  | Customer Experience |

## 3. Pricing and Discount Approval

### Business Scenario

A retail chain implementing a dynamic pricing and discount management system.

### Process Overview

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/uc-retail-flow-3.svg" alt="Flowchart of a pricing approval process: Pricing Proposal routed by discount level (standard, high or strategic) to Automated Approval, Management Review or Executive Approval, then Price Update, Marketing Communication, Inventory Adjustment and Sales Channel Update, with rejected proposals returned for revision" className="block dark:hidden" />
  <img src="/images/uc-retail-flow-3-dark.svg" alt="Flowchart of a pricing approval process: Pricing Proposal routed by discount level (standard, high or strategic) to Automated Approval, Management Review or Executive Approval, then Price Update, Marketing Communication, Inventory Adjustment and Sales Channel Update, with rejected proposals returned for revision" className="hidden dark:block" />

### Key Process Details

| Stage               | Description                | Duration | Key Actors                  |
| ------------------- | -------------------------- | -------- | --------------------------- |
| Pricing Proposal    | Initial pricing strategy   | 1-2 days | Pricing Team                |
| Approval Process    | Management review          | 2-3 days | Various Management Levels   |
| Implementation      | Price and inventory update | 1-2 days | Pricing and Inventory Teams |
| Marketing Alignment | Communication strategy     | 1-2 days | Marketing Department        |

## Business Value Proposition

These use cases demonstrate how process automation can:
- Reduce order processing time by 60-80%
- Improve customer satisfaction and loyalty
- Enhance pricing flexibility and responsiveness
- Minimize human error in critical retail processes
- Provide real-time visibility into operational workflows
- Standardize complaint resolution procedures
- Accelerate decision-making in pricing and discounts
- Optimize inventory management
- Enable data-driven business strategies
- Improve overall operational efficiency
