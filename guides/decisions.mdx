---
title: "Decisions"
description: "Author, test, and deploy decision models."
---

Decision models capture business logic as inputs, rules, and outputs that can be
versioned, tested, and governed independently of your application code.

## Authoring

Model your decision logic, then validate it against representative test cases
before promoting it to a shared environment.

<Tip>
  Keep decision logic out of application code so business users can review and
  change rules without a deployment.
</Tip>
